Soapbox Science Brighton 2024

11am - 2pm
Saturday 1st June, 2024


Brighton & Hove beach

By the i360

Soapbox Science Brighton will be returning to Brighton & Hove beach on Saturday 1st June 2024, and the call for volunteers is now open!

Please let us know if you are interested in helping us to ensure that the event runs smoothly on the day - we would greatly appreciate your help! We will begin recruiting volunteers to help in April 2024.

We need help on the day with speaker support, evaluation, crowd management, photography, social media, and set up and take down between 10am and 3pm.

If you are interested in helping on the day, please complete the online form here. A soapbox science t-shirt and vegetarian packed lunch will be provided on the day.

Deadline for volunteering is the end of the day (midnight) on Friday 10th May 2024.

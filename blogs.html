The power of collaboration and diversity in interdisciplinary research: in the case of a responsible gambling project



Read Ruijie's blog post

Ruijie is a Soapbox Science Brighton speaker for 2022, an interdisciplinary researcher and a Lecturer in Psychology based at Bournemouth University. Her research interest lies in Cyberpsychology and Human Computer Interaction. As a woman working in STEM disciplines, Ruijie will be talking about “Being 'GambleAware': How science and tech can help and why collaboration matters” at Brighton seafront for Soapbox Science Brighton, 2022.

An expedition into the world of sub-atomic particles



Read Charanjit's blog post

Meet Charanjit Kaur Khosa who will be speaking at Soapbox Science Brighton 2022, describing the current questions in theoretical particle physics and approaches being used to solve them. These methods developed in particle physics also can solve problems in other areas like global warming and epidemiology.

Charanjit is keen to share the joy of science with young students, and to inspire young women to pursue science and push the frontiers of scientific knowledge. At the moment, we need more woman participation in science to bridge the gender gap in STEM fields, which will also help bring equality in all other facets of society.

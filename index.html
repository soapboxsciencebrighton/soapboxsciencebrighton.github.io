Soapbox Science organises events across the UK and the world where women scientists talk about the science that they do at a public venue. These events celebrate women in STEMM and challenge the stereotypes about who STEMM researchers are.

Soapbox Science Brighton began back in 2017, and is organised by a team at the University of Sussex.

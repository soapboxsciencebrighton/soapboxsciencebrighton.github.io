Soapbox Science Brighton 2025

11am - 2pm
Saturday 24th May, 2025


Brighton & Hove beach

By the i360

Soapbox Science Brighton 2025 was held on Brighton & Hove beach on Saturday 24th May 2025. While we had grey skies and a strong wind, that didn't dampen the spirit of the passers-by eager for some science!

Below are all the details about the speakers and topics presented at the 2025 event.
